SCENE 5

Name of Song Chosen

The sun shines into your eyes as you step out of your apartment building, guitar case slung over your shoulder. The black hole, which stays the size of a tennis ball nowadays, follows eagerly at your feet like an anxious puppy.

You raise a hand above your face to block the light before you start your journey down the street, in search of a change in scenery that'll inspire you to create some new music.

As you skate past the old cafe you used to work at, you slow down and take a look through the perfectly polished windows. The Alexas are making drinks and cleaning the tables like usual, and a third robot— your replacement, a voice in your head says—stands next to your old manager behind the register. He's showing the robot how to take a customer's order with a friendly smile, but you've never seen him look quite so tired.

You try to push away your intrusive thoughts; you're much better off now than when you left the cafe three months ago. It doesn't matter who replaced you.

You're still curious about how the cafe's doing though, so you figure it wouldn't hurt to stop for a cup of coffee.

Your manager looks up as you push open the familiar doors of the cafe. His eyes brighten, and his face breaks into a tentative smile.

"Hey, Alex! What brings you here today?" he asks.

You shrug, suddenly feeling a little awkward.
