Along the Way

Along the Way is the choose your own adventure story of twenty-two-year-old Alex (they/them) who lives in a world overrun by robots, and not the cool Iron Man kind. Instead, the bots are boring old service bots, brewing drinks and mopping floors. While Alex works at a local cafe as a barista—the only job they could get after college—the robots begin to replace all of their human coworkers, the only ones who were able to keep Alex sane during the hustle and bustle of the store.
